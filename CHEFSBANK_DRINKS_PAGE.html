Hurricane Cocktail

Duration: 5 minutes
Ingredients: 50ml dark rum, 50ml white rum, 1 passion fruit, 1 orange juice, 1 lemon juice, 50ml sugar syrup, 2 tbsp grenadine, 4 cocktail cherries, 2 orange slices
Preparation
1. Fill a cocktail shaker with ice then add the rums. Scoop the flesh and seeds from the passion fruit and add to the shaker along with the orange and lemon juices, sugar syrup and grenadine.
2. Shake well until the outside of the cocktail shaker feels icy cold. Fill two hurricane glasses with fresh ice then double strain the drink into the prepared glasses.
3. Garnish each one with an orange slice skewered onto a cocktail stick and a couple of cocktail cherries.

Cranberry and Raspberry Smoothie

Duration: 10 minutes
Ingredients: 200ml cranberry juice, 75g frozen raspberry defrosted , 100ml milk, 200ml natural yogurt, 1 tbsp caster sugar , or to taste, mint sprigs, to serve
Preparation
Place all the ingredients into a blender and pulse until smooth. Pour into glasses and serve topped with fresh mint.

Oreo Milkshake

Duration: 12 minutes
Ingredients: 2 Cups Vanilla Ice Cream, ⅔ Cup Milk, 8 Oreo Cookies, 1 Teaspoon Vanilla Extract Optional,Whipped Cream for serving Optional
Preparation
1. Add the ice cream, milk and Oreo cookies to your blender. Pulse until mostly smooth.
2. Serve in large glasses with whipped cream if desired.
